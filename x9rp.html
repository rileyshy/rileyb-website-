X9 Roleplay

Welcome to X9RP

X9RP is an exceptional British roleplay server on FiveM, designed to offer a highly immersive and engaging experience for players. X9RP stands out as a top-tier community for those passionate about roleplaying in a realistic and meticulously crafted environment.

Server Overview

What is X9RP?

X9RP is a FiveM server that provides a robust British roleplay environment where players can create and develop their unique stories. Whether you aspire to enforce the law, engage in business ventures, or navigate the complexities of a criminal life, X9RP offers endless possibilities for all types of roleplayers.

Ownership and Management

The server is expertly managed by Hayes, a dedicated owner committed to ensuring that X9RP maintains its high standards and remains a welcoming community for all players.

Key Features

Authentic British Setting

X9RP prides itself on providing an authentic British roleplay experience. The server's custom maps, vehicles, and assets are meticulously chosen to reflect the look and feel of modern Britain.

Diverse Roleplay Opportunities

Whether you’re interested in law enforcement, healthcare, business, or the underworld of crime, X9RP offers a wide array of career paths and opportunities:

  • Law Enforcement: Join the police force and help maintain law and order.
  • Emergency Services: Become part of the medical or fire rescue teams.
  • Civilian Life: Engage in various civilian roles or explore the criminal underworld.
  • Special Events: Participate in server-wide events and storylines.

Community and Support

X9RP boasts a vibrant community of roleplayers who are passionate about creating engaging stories. The server’s Discord is a hub for interaction, where players can connect, share their experiences, and get support. The community is known for its friendliness and inclusivity, welcoming both new and seasoned roleplayers.

Joining X9RP

How to Get Started

1. Install FiveM: Ensure you have FiveM installed on your PC. Download FiveM from the official website.

2. Connect to the Server: Use the CFX link to join directly: X9RP on FiveM.

3. Join the Discord: Engage with the community, get updates, and access support by joining the X9RP Discord Server.
