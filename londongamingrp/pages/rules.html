#1 London Gaming Rules

1. General Conduct:
Respect all members of the community. Discrimination, racism, harassment, or any form of bullying is strictly prohibited.
No toxic behavior or excessive arguing in OOC chat. Keep all disputes respectful and use the proper channels (e.g., support tickets, Discord) to resolve issues.
Follow the chain of command when dealing with staff issues or disputes.
2. Roleplay Standards:
Serious roleplay is expected at all times. Always stay in character and avoid breaking RP unless it's absolutely necessary (e.g., server-wide emergencies).
No random Deathmatch (RDM). Engaging in violent actions without a valid RP reason is not allowed.
No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM). Intentionally using vehicles as weapons outside of valid RP scenarios is forbidden.
Value your life (VLRP). Always act as though your character values their life. Do not engage in situations that your character would realistically avoid.
Powergaming and metagaming are strictly prohibited. Do not use out-of-character knowledge or force unrealistic actions to gain an advantage.
3. In-Game Behavior:
Do not force actions on other players without giving them a fair chance to respond (e.g., "/me knocks out the person" should give the other player a chance to react).
No combat logging. Logging out of the server to avoid an RP situation or consequences is a serious offense.
Criminal activities should be realistic and fit within the lore of your character. Excessive or unrealistic crime sprees will lead to consequences.
Police and Emergency Services must be respected. If you're in a situation where you're arrested or detained, continue to roleplay as realistically as possible.
