ABOUT Luxury Gaming Roleplay

Why Join Luxury Gaming RP?

#1 British Based Server

Luxury Gaming Roleplay Was Founded in July 2024, With the Intent to Provide a Steady and Enjoyable Experience for the Growing Community. From the Start, We Have Done Exactly That. After 6+ Months of UK Roleplay V1, It Was Decided That a Fresh Start Would Be Nice and Would Allow the Community to Fight for the Riches and See Who Would Excel in Their Chosen Path. V2 Ran for Over a Year, Reaching Over 10,000 Connected Players and a Still Increasing Discord of 2,500 Members. Now That V3 Was in the Making, Our Team Were Able to Focus and Make Huge Changes to the Server, Rebuilding It From the Ground Up, Using a Newer Framework. After Over a Month of Hard Work, V3 Was Ready to Launch and the Communities Excitement Began to Rise. Upon the Launch of V3, We Saw the Community Rush to Join the Server and Create Their Character, to Get Into the Open World as Fast as Possible to Being Trying Out and Enjoying the Many New Additions of V3. These Ranged From; Pool, Darts, Carnival Games, Working Roller Coasters, and a Whole Lot More. Now the Community is Stronger Than Ever, With the Server Constantly Having a Steady Number of Players With Our Amazing Staff Team Assisting Anyone and Everyone as Best as They Can.
